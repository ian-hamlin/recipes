# Nandos Macho Peas

## Notes

> Makes 6 servings

## Ingredients

- 400g frozen peas
- Water
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Bunch of fresh parsley, finely chopped
- Bunch of fresh mint, finely chopped
- Salt
- Chili flakes

## Directions

Boil the peas, and once they're cooked pour the water away but leave the peas in the pan.

Stir in the oil, salt, chili and herbs.
